Discover Kirinyaga's Hidden Gem

Experience Authentic Kenya

Nestled in the beautiful landscapes of Kirinyaga County, Cloud 9 Bed & Breakfast offers a unique blend of modern comfort and authentic Kenyan culture. Our location along the Nairobi-Nyeri Highway makes us the perfect stopover for travelers exploring Central Kenya.

Kirinyaga County is known for its fertile lands, beautiful scenery, and rich cultural heritage. From our doorstep, you can explore Mount Kenya's foothills, visit local coffee and tea plantations, or simply enjoy the tranquil atmosphere of the Kenyan countryside.

At Cloud 9, we take pride in offering genuine Kenyan hospitality with a touch of luxury. Our staff are local experts who can help you plan excursions to nearby attractions and ensure your stay is truly memorable.
